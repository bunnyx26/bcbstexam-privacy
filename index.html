1. Introduction

BC BST Exam Trainer (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the mobile application BC BST Exam Trainer (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

  • Advertising Data: We show ads in the App via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect non-personally-identifiable information such as your device’s advertising identifier (e.g., IDFA on iOS or AAID on Android) and anonymous usage data to serve you personalized ads.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data (name, email, location, or other identifiers) beyond what AdMob automatically collects for ad serving.

3. How We Use Information

We use the data collected by AdMob solely to deliver and improve ad experiences. We do not use any data for our own analytics, marketing, or other purposes.

4. Analytics

We do not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, etc.). We have no analytics data collection beyond the ad-serving identifiers used by AdMob.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share any personal information with third parties. AdMob’s handling of advertising identifiers is governed by Google’s policies, not by us.

6. Your Choices

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: On Android, you can opt out via your device’s Ads Settings. On iOS, you can limit ad tracking in Settings → Privacy → Tracking.
  • Delete App Data: Uninstalling the App will remove any stored app data, including locally cached preferences.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes acceptance.
